An artisan experience 65 years in the making

In 1952, Giueseppe Giacosa started the first Brick Oven Pizza Co. in Brooklyn with a loan from his grandmother. He believed that with top-notch ingredients and a little Sicilian know-how, he could make the best pizza in the borough. Millions of satisfied customers later, his legacy lives on.
